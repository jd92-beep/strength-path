# Strength Path

## Summary
Mobile-first guided strength training web app. Turns the open exercises dataset (1,324 moves with GIFs + step instructions) into a clear path: learn form → train body parts → complete progressive workouts.

## Who
Beginners and returning trainees who want to get stronger but do not know what to do next. Primary device: phone in a gym or at home between sets.

## Platform
web

## Register
product

## Core jobs
1. Follow a step-by-step strength path (Foundation → Dumbbell → Barbell).
2. Learn any exercise with animation + numbered coaching steps.
3. Browse and train by body part.
4. Run a guided workout session (sets, rest, next move).
